Background On Company

I realized the lack of knowledge that the community has when it involves the rental criteria. I have witnessed countless evictions, kept security deposits, outstanding balances, and over 11,500 people and counting experiencing homelessness on a daily basis. After Years of working within the property management community, I was able to gain the knowledge that will help the community throughout the entire nation. I became experienced with Fair Housing, GraceHill, and Franklin County Eviction Court. I am currently working with County Children services throughout Ohio to implement an Eviction Prevention Program to ensure that Individuals & families can strive for stability and overcome the barriers of Housing needs and concerns. Mission and Vision Statement

Our mission is to educate individuals on how to maintain a good status within their rental history, We strive to give you the tools to become a homeowner. By offering individuals the best advice and the resources that will put you on the right path to climb your way out of poverty and on the road to family success! We Will Find The Best Solution Possible!
TRG Foundation purpose is to help end poverty throughout the world, one person at a time. Our vision is to change the statistics of the world views on poverty. We believe that it takes a village to obtain real life changing success, and we will strive to achieve financial freedom, and great living stability.
